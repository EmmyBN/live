ProtoKlenz GT ProtoKlenz GT-SC is a revolutionary new “Super-Concentrated” gas turbine compressor wash fluid, with a 19:1 dilution ratio, making it 4 times more concentrated than standard 4:1 products.


This equates to savings on transportation, packaging, storage and a significantly reduced environmental impact.


75% Less Waste

The high concentration means you only need 1 container for every 4 required with a standard, 4:1 dilution, product.


This means financial savings on transportation and packaging. It also means greater convenience as there will be less containers to move about, store and ultimately dispose of.


ProtoKlenz GT-SC is a simple way to save money, eliminate waste and reduce your carbon footprint.


Simple

Our latest advancement in cleaning chemical technology, means you can enjoy all the great features and cleaning power of our standard ProtoKlenz GT for less.


There is no additional cost to use this product. ProtoKlenz GT-SC simply needs to be diluted in the same way as any other compressor washing chemical, using OEM compliant demineralised water.


It is available in a range of packaging from 25 litres, up to 1,000 litre reusable IBCs.
