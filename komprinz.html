--- title: KompRinz ---
Cold Weather
Wash & Rinse Fluid

KompRinz is a ready-to-use substitute for demineralised water, designed specifically for washing gas turbine compressors in freezing conditions. It is used both for diluting the cleaning chemical and for rinsing. The carefully controlled formulation means it is a perfect replacement for demineralised water, where cold temperatures require the use of anti-freeze additives that still comply with the strict criteria for purity and contaminants.


KompRinz is an economical and safe solution, providing operators with complete piece of mind that both off-line and on-line washing in cold conditions can be safely performed without the risk of damage to their gas turbines. It is also biodegradable and non-toxic, so does not pose any risk of harm to workers or to the environment.


Normally water and water-based cleaning solutions will freeze as their temperature reaches 0°C. Factors such as the cooling effect of fast moving air mean that freezing can actually occur when the ambient air temperature is 5-12°C higher. Using KompRinz instead of water means the freezing point of the fluids used for washing and rinsing will be safely and accurately depressed, ensuring fluidity is maintained.


KompRinz works perfectly with ProtoKlenz GT and also with other compressor washing fluids.

Technical Specification
Appearance Colourless clear liquid
Odour No characteristic odour
Density at 0°C 1.0 Sg. (Typical)
Boiling Point > 100°C
Flash Point None to boiling
Freeze Point -17°C
Water Solubility Completely soluble

Packaging

1,000 litre (IBC) plastic containers.


Usage

Diluting ProtoKlenz GT at a ratio of 4:1, results in a wash solution suitable for compressor inlet temperatures down to -13°C, (and for rinsing down to -17°C). Lower freeze point versions of KompRinz are available on request.
